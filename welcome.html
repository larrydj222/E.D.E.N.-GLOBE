Welcome to MAP E.D.E.N.
Earth Diametric Energy Network • Globe Simulator + Switchboard

MAP E.D.E.N. is the operational globe interface for Project E.D.E.N. — a measurement-first field program centered on repeatable testing along defined Earth corridors, beginning with the 42° / 222° diametric axis.

How to use the globe

Open the drawer and tap Switchboard to reveal the layer controls. Each layer toggles a visual overlay on the globe. Use drag to rotate, pinch to zoom, and the drawer can be collapsed/expanded for more screen space.

Mission

Turn the 42° / 222° axis hypothesis into a coordinated, evidence-first experiment by producing sensor datasets that are repeatable, controlled, and comparable across stations and time.

MAP E.D.E.N. equips trusted operators with clear protocols to collect environmental field measurements and navigation-behavior observations so results can be compared across locations, devices, and sessions.
